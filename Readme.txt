Installation:
Download a release of the mod and unzip it to the Mods folder in the Synth Riders directory.

Setup:
In the unzipped folder there should be a template.txt and a status.txt
You can edit the template.txt and put together the information you want to display.
The status.txt will be a filled out version of the template.txt once a song has started.

Commands:
<name>
<mapper>
<artist>
<bpm>
<difficulty>
<misses>
<mode>
<noFailMode>
<limitedEnergy>
<accuracy>
<obstacles>
<noteJumpdSpeed>
<noteSize>
<colliderSize>
<rainbowMode>
<haloNotes>
